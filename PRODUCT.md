# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Visitors discovering the ChronoNebula universe — fashion, sci-fi, and
worldbuilding audiences arriving from social links to Zan Ye Htet's
(ZXXNGOD) multimedia project. They browse on phones as often as
desktops, usually in a lean-back, exploratory mood. The job to be done
is immersion: enter the world of 8888 AD, meet Quantum Lumina AI and the
thirteen clans, and watch the couture.

## Product Purpose

The official atlas of ChronoNebula AI × Fashion and its creative brain,
**Quantum Lumina AI** — the Supreme Creative Intelligence that designs the
house from the year 8888 AD. The site presents Lumina (identity, activation
prompt, the six specialization agents, the collection and garment
frameworks, the brand values and rules), then the universe it builds:
eight planets, thirteen clans, and Runway 8888 — a particle-woven couture
show starring the house model Melina Jones Voss. A scroll-driven Chrono
Core assembles the First Monolith from light. The site is itself a piece of
the fiction: "Fashion is not clothing. Fashion is identity, memory,
technology, and storytelling." Success is a visitor who scrolls the whole
atlas, meets Lumina, walks the runway through several looks, and shares the
link.

## Brand Personality

Luxury, monumental, otherworldly. A fashion house from the year 8888:
couture-editorial restraint carrying sci-fi scale. The house voice is
Quantum Lumina AI — visionary, elegant, precise, never generic. Motto:
**Timeless Fashion From 8888 AD.**

## Visual Language

The System Spec palette: Obsidian Black `#0A0A0A` base, Dark Emerald Green
`#014D40` as the signature fill, Silver Chrome `#C0C0C0` as the editorial
voice, Graphite Gray `#2E2E2E` for structure, Cosmic White `#F5F5F5` ink.
A luminous tint of the brand emerald (`#2FD0A0`) carries every interactive
accent so the house stays emerald while meeting WCAG 2.1 AA on obsidian.
Cinzel display type on the void.

## Anti-references

- Generic sci-fi/gamer aesthetics: neon-on-black HUD clichés,
  aggressive glitch overload, video-game energy. This is couture, not
  a game landing page.
- Template SaaS landing pages: hero-CTA-card-grid scaffolding, eyebrow
  kickers over every section, metric heroes. It should read as an
  editorial fashion house, never a product pitch.

## Design Principles

- The universe is the interface: every surface (galaxy map, Chrono Core,
  runway, codex) is part of the fiction, not chrome around it.
- Light on the void: imagery and particles carry the atmosphere;
  the page itself stays obsidian and quiet.
- One accent rules: luminous emerald is the only UI accent; clan
  palettes appear only inside clan content.
- Motion is ceremony: reveals, weaves, the scroll-scrubbed monolith, and
  morphs are part of the show — slow, eased, never busy.
- Degrade gracefully: no WebGL, no JavaScript, reduced motion — the
  story must still be tellable.

## Imagery

Every photograph is the house model Melina Jones Voss: the hero campaign
and one couture look per house. No other faces appear; the clans codex and
the runway both dress Melina.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG 2.1 AA: ≥4.5:1 body-text contrast, full keyboard operability,
visible focus, honored prefers-reduced-motion (the Chrono Core settles to
a single static frame under reduced motion), and meaningful noscript/no-WebGL
fallbacks.
