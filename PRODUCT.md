# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Visitors discovering the ChronoNebula universe — fashion, sci-fi, and
worldbuilding audiences arriving from social links to Zan Ye Htet's
(ZXXNGOD) multimedia project. They browse on phones as often as
desktops, usually in a lean-back, exploratory mood. The job to be done
is immersion: enter the world of 8888 AD, meet the thirteen clans, and
watch the couture.

## Product Purpose

The official atlas of the ChronoNebula AI × Fashion universe: eight
planets, thirteen clans, and Runway 8888 — a particle-woven couture
show starring the house model Melina Jones Voss. The site is itself a
piece of the fiction: "fashion is no longer clothing, fashion is
civilization." Success is a visitor who scrolls the whole atlas, walks
the runway through several looks, and shares the link.

## Brand Personality

Luxury, monumental, otherworldly. A fashion house from the year 8888:
couture-editorial restraint carrying sci-fi scale. Every clan has an
official Codex palette; the house accent is Nebula Emerald on Deep
Void black with Cinzel display type.

## Anti-references

- Generic sci-fi/gamer aesthetics: neon-on-black HUD clichés,
  aggressive glitch overload, video-game energy. This is couture, not
  a game landing page.
- Template SaaS landing pages: hero-CTA-card-grid scaffolding, eyebrow
  kickers over every section, metric heroes. It should read as an
  editorial fashion house, never a product pitch.

## Design Principles

- The universe is the interface: every surface (galaxy map, runway,
  codex) is part of the fiction, not chrome around it.
- Light on the void: imagery and particles carry the atmosphere;
  the page itself stays deep black and quiet.
- One accent rules: Nebula Emerald is the only UI accent; clan
  palettes appear only inside clan content.
- Motion is ceremony: reveals, weaves, and morphs are part of the
  show — slow, eased, never busy.
- Degrade gracefully: no WebGL, no JavaScript, reduced motion — the
  story must still be tellable.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG 2.1 AA: ≥4.5:1 body-text contrast, full keyboard operability,
visible focus, honored prefers-reduced-motion (already partially in
place), and meaningful noscript/no-WebGL fallbacks.
